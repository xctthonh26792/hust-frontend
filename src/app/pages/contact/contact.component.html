Knowledge hub

Vision: The Knowledge Hub is established to provide the small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in particular, and other stakeholders in common, a vibrant interaction platform that meets requirements of adopting Circular Economy (CE) philosophy in small and medium enterprises’ supply chain in Vietnam and create a culture of collaboration among stakeholders for development of CE in Vietnam

Mission: Knowledge Hub has been created:

  • To connect the SMEs with stakeholders (researchers, academia institution, education institution and policymakers) in Vietnam and UK
  • To share information among the stakeholders and exchange the experience on adopting CE philosophy in SMEs
  • To discuss on problems, ideas and opportunities for development of CE in businesses in Vietnam.

Strategy:

  • Wider engagement of stakeholders
  • Create and maintain a vibrant research and discussion environment
  • Form actual collaboration between SMEs and academia institutions based on the demand
  • Promote the best actual CE model to the SMEs
  • Connect the platform with other research hubs

Content:

  • Create a network of stakeholders with different levels
  • Provide and share information
  • Provide literature resources (book, paper, project, case study, web link)
  • Provide service products (online training course or meeting, online consultancy, research and development opportunities, etc.)
  • Create forums for information sharing and ideas exchanging
  • Link with other services and website from formal collaboration
  • Advertising in term of CE
